About

      I’m Alan Wang, a first year PhD student researching hardware security at the University of California, Berkeley (UCB) advised by Profesor Chris Fletcher. My main focus is side channel attacks and defenses (hardware and software). Specifically, I focus on Spectre attacks and mobile microarchitectural security. I occasionally will dabble in computer systems research. Outside of research, I play soccer, swim, and poorly play volleyball.
