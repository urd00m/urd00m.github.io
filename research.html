Research Interests

      My research interests lie in hardware/systems security with a side on computer systems. Recently, my work has been focused on side-channel vulnerabilities inherent to graphics stacks and discovering new microarchitectural side-channel attack techniques. I’m currently advised by Professor Chris Fletcher at the University of California, Berkeley.
